# Financial Simulators — Full Content

## SIP Simulator
Projects SIP growth with inflation adjustment and LTCG tax. Default: ₹10K/month for 20 years at 12% return with 6% inflation. Shows: nominal corpus, real value, tax impact, effective return rate.

## EMI Analyzer
Compares loan EMIs across tenures. Shows total interest paid vs principal, and the real cost of a loan after inflation. Includes prepayment impact analysis.

## Tax Sandbox
Side-by-side old vs new tax regime comparison. Includes HRA exemption, 80C/80D deductions, LTCG/STCG tax, surcharge and cess. Optimizes for lowest tax automatically.
