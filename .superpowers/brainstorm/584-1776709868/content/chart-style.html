Chart Structure: How to Show the Divergence

The core visualization must make the gap between Nominal and Real wealth unmistakable. Which chart structure serves the "anti-marketing" truth-telling mission best?

Stacked Area — The "Gap" Visualizer

Two filled areas with the gap between them highlighted. The divergence zone itself becomes the story. Stress test shown as a red-shaded zone at the end of the timeline. Best for showing how the gap widens over time.

Line Chart — The "Clarity" Approach

Clean lines with stress scenarios as dashed red paths. Year markers on X-axis. More precise, less emotional. Better for comparing exact values. Stress shown as parallel dashed lines branching from the base curves.

My Recommendation: Stacked Area ("Gap" Visualizer)

The filled area between Nominal and Real makes the inflation erosion viscerally visible — you literally SEE the wealth disappearing. A line chart shows the same data but the emotional impact is muted. For an anti-marketing tool, the visual shock IS the feature.
